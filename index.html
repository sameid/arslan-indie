BIO

Arslan is an individual who strives to empower others, strengthen their physical and mental well-being, and create meaningful relationships within his community. He advocates for living life free of fear and to your complete ability.

A bit of a hipster, Arslan tries to enjoy life and all the experience it brings forth while constantly looking for the magic in every moment.

Currently, Arslan works as a Personal Trainer in the Greater Toronto Area in Ontario and is a lifestyle blogger.
